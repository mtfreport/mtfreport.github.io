🌏 Research and Support for Transfeminine Youth in China

MtF.Report is a small group dedicated to researching and assisting transfeminine youth in China. We document their real situations, publish reports, and accept donations to directly support their lives, healthcare and safety.

MtF.Report 是一个致力于研究和援助中国青少年跨性别女性（MtF）的小型团体。我们记录她们的真实处境，撰写报告，并接受捐款以直接支持她们的生活、医疗与安全。

🧭 为什么建立这个项目？ / Why Start This Project?

中国的许多跨性别女性，尤其是青少年，因身份暴露而遭遇家庭暴力、校园欺凌、心理创伤与医疗歧视。她们往往难以对外发声，更难获得国际社会的理解与帮助。

Many trans women in China, especially young people, face domestic violence, bullying, trauma and medical discrimination when their identity is exposed. They rarely have a voice and it is even harder to get understanding or help from the international community.

MtF.Report 旨在打破语言与信息的壁垒，让世界看见她们的处境。

🔍 我们提供什么？ / What Do We Offer?

  • 📄 研究报告：聚焦中国 MtF 青少年处境的专题与统计分析
    Research reports focusing on the situation of Chinese MtF youth
  • 📅 事件时间线：记录公共事件与个人案例的动态
    Event timelines covering public incidents and personal stories
  • 🤝 援助方式：开放捐助通道，接受志愿者申请，发布行动指南
    Ways to help: donation channels, volunteer sign‑ups and action guides
